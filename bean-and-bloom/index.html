Our Story

Bean & Bloom started with a simple idea: what if your morning coffee came with a breath of fresh air — literally?

In 2019, we transformed a neglected corner lot into something unexpected — a cafe wrapped in a living garden. The walls are lined with trailing pothos, the tables sit between citrus trees, and the espresso machine hums beside a wall of ferns.

We source our beans from small-batch roasters who care about the farmers as much as the flavor. Our pastries are baked in-house every morning before dawn. And our garden? It grows the herbs for our seasonal specials — from the rosemary in our focaccia to the lavender in our lattes.

This isn't just a coffee shop. It's a place to slow down, breathe deep, and remember that good things grow when you give them space.
