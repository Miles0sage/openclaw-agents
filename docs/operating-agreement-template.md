# OPERATING AGREEMENT

## [COMPANY NAME] LLC

**This Operating Agreement is entered into as of [DATE]**

---

## I. FORMATION AND NAME

1.1 **Formation.** [COMPANY NAME] LLC (the "Company") is a single-member limited liability company organized and existing under the laws of the State of Arizona, pursuant to the Arizona Revised Statutes Title 29 (Limited Liability Company Act).

1.2 **Name.** The Company's name is [COMPANY NAME] LLC. The Company may conduct business under any assumed name(s) as determined by the Member.

1.3 **Principal Place of Business.** The Company's principal place of business is located at [ADDRESS], or such other location as the Member may designate.

1.4 **Registered Agent.** The Company's registered agent in Arizona is [REGISTERED AGENT NAME], located at [REGISTERED AGENT ADDRESS].

---

## II. PURPOSE AND BUSINESS SCOPE

2.1 **Purpose.** The Company is organized for the purpose of engaging in the business of:

- Artificial intelligence software development
- Business automation services
- SaaS (Software-as-a-Service) products
- Technology consulting and advisory services
- Digital product development and licensing
- Any other lawful business activities as determined by the Member

  2.2 **Powers.** The Company shall have all the powers of a limited liability company under Arizona law, including the power to own, purchase, lease, or otherwise acquire property; to borrow money; to execute contracts; and to conduct any business activities necessary or incidental to the stated purpose.

---

## III. MEMBERSHIP AND OWNERSHIP

3.1 **Member.** Miles ("Member") is the sole member and 100% owner of the Company.

3.2 **Liability Limitation.** The Member's liability is limited to the capital contributions made to the Company, except as provided by law.

3.3 **Capital Contributions.** The Member shall contribute capital to the Company as needed to fund operations and business objectives. Initial capital contributions are as follows:

- Cash: $[AMOUNT] (or such amount as the Member determines)

  3.4 **Capital Account.** The Member's capital account shall be maintained reflecting all capital contributions, distributions, and allocations of profit/loss.

---

## IV. DISTRIBUTIONS AND ALLOCATIONS

4.1 **Allocation of Income and Loss.** All net income, losses, and deductions of the Company shall be allocated 100% to the Member.

4.2 **Distributions.** The Member may withdraw distributions of Company profits as determined by the Member, subject to:

- Sufficient liquidity and working capital requirements
- Tax liability obligations
- Any debt obligations or liabilities of the Company

  4.3 **Timing.** Distributions shall be made at such times and in such amounts as the Member determines in the Member's sole discretion.

  4.4 **No Obligation to Contribute.** The Member shall have no obligation to contribute additional capital beyond agreed amounts, except as otherwise determined by the Member.

---

## V. MANAGEMENT AND OPERATIONS

5.1 **Member-Managed.** The Company shall be member-managed. The Member shall have full authority to manage, control, and operate the Company's business and affairs.

5.2 **Powers and Duties.** The Member shall have the authority to:

- Execute contracts and agreements on behalf of the Company
- Open and maintain bank accounts
- Borrow money on behalf of the Company
- Buy, sell, or lease Company property
- Hire and terminate employees and contractors
- Set compensation and benefits
- Make all business decisions and determinations
- Delegate duties to agents, employees, or contractors

  5.3 **Decisions and Approvals.** All management decisions, approvals, and determinations shall rest solely with the Member. No approval from other parties is required.

  5.4 **Meetings.** The Member may hold meetings as deemed necessary. Formal meetings are not required.

  5.5 **Books and Records.** The Company shall maintain accurate books of account, records of all transactions, and all documents required by law. Records shall be available to the Member at all times.

---

## VI. FISCAL MATTERS

6.1 **Fiscal Year.** The Company's fiscal year shall be the calendar year (January 1 through December 31).

6.2 **Banking.** The Member shall maintain Company bank accounts and investment accounts as necessary for Company operations.

6.3 **Tax Elections.** The Company shall file all required federal and state tax returns. The Member shall determine appropriate tax elections under the Internal Revenue Code.

6.4 **Tax Treatment.** As a single-member LLC, the Company shall be treated as a disregarded entity for federal tax purposes unless the Member elects otherwise.

---

## VII. INDEMNIFICATION AND LIABILITY

7.1 **Indemnification.** The Company shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the Member against all claims, damages, losses, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising from:

- Actions taken in the Member's capacity as manager
- Business decisions made in good faith
- Company operations and obligations
- Third-party claims against the Company

  7.2 **Insurance.** The Company may obtain liability, errors and omissions, and other insurance coverage as determined by the Member to protect Company assets and operations.

  7.3 **Limitation of Liability.** The Member's liability for Company debts and obligations shall be limited to the Company's assets, except as provided by law or when the Member personally guarantees obligations.

---

## VIII. DISSOLUTION AND TERMINATION

8.1 **Dissolution Events.** The Company shall be dissolved upon:

- Decision by the Member to dissolve the Company
- Withdrawal or death of the Member (unless continued by action of the Member or designated successor)
- Judgment of dissolution by a court of competent jurisdiction
- Occurrence of any event that makes the Company's business unlawful

  8.2 **Dissolution Procedure.** Upon dissolution, the Member shall:

- Wind up the Company's business and affairs
- Collect and liquidate Company assets
- Pay all Company debts and liabilities
- Distribute remaining assets to the Member

  8.3 **Continuation After Member Death.** Upon the Member's death, the Company shall immediately dissolve unless the Member's estate, beneficiaries, or successors elect to continue the Company within 90 days.

---

## IX. AMENDMENT AND MODIFICATION

9.1 **Amendment.** This Operating Agreement may be amended, modified, or supplemented by written instrument executed by the Member. No amendment shall be effective without the Member's written consent.

9.2 **Restatement.** This Operating Agreement may be restated in whole or in part at the Member's discretion.

---

## X. MISCELLANEOUS

10.1 **Governing Law.** This Operating Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Arizona, without regard to conflicts of law principles.

10.2 **Entire Agreement.** This Operating Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties regarding the Company and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and agreements, whether written or oral.

10.3 **Severability.** If any provision of this Operating Agreement is found to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

10.4 **Notices.** All notices required under this Agreement shall be in writing and delivered personally, by email, or by certified mail to the address(es) set forth herein.

10.5 **No Third-Party Beneficiaries.** This Operating Agreement is intended solely for the benefit of the Company and the Member and creates no rights for any third parties.

10.6 **Waiver.** No waiver of any provision or breach of this Agreement shall be valid unless in writing and signed by the Member. Waiver of any breach shall not constitute waiver of any other or subsequent breach.

10.7 **Counterparts.** This Operating Agreement may be executed in one or more counterparts, each deemed an original and all together constituting one and the same instrument.
