Our Story

Bean & Bloom started as a dream shared over a cup of coffee in a tiny greenhouse. We believed that the best conversations happen surrounded by living things — the scent of fresh soil, the green of trailing pothos, the warmth of a perfectly pulled espresso.

In 2021, we opened our doors in the heart of the neighborhood with a simple mission: create a space where coffee culture meets botanical beauty. Every plant you see is tended by hand. Every drink is crafted with ingredients inspired by the garden around you.

We source our beans from small-batch roasters who share our commitment to sustainability. Our pastries are baked in-house each morning. And our greenhouse seating area? It's our favorite spot too.
